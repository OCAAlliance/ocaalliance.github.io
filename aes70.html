AES70

The public standard specification of OCA is the AES70 standard, published by the Audio Engineering Society, or AES. AES70 is an open, license-free specification that may be freely used at no cost.

The current version of the standard is AES70-2018.

The AES70 Standards Suite

Unified Modeling Language (UML)

AES70-2 Annex A and AES70-3 Annex B are Unified Modeling Language (UML) specifications. AES70-2 Annex A is particularly important, as it contains the entire specification of the AES70 Class Structure.

The UML files are in two forms:

  • The *.eap files are master files from a UML tool named Enterprise Architect from Sparx Systems. The usual version of the tool costs approximately US$230, but Sparx Systems offers a free viewer. There is also a 30-day free trial version of the full package. Both of these products are available from the Sparx Systems download page here.
  • The *.xmi files are master files in XMI 2.1, a standard format for representation of UML information. XML stands for "XML Metadata Interchange". XMI files can be opened by most UML editors, including free ones. See this Wikipedia article for more information.
