Introduction

The OCA Alliance Technical Site aims to share technical resources and information with people working with AES70. The site itself is curated by members of the OCA Alliance.

Public AES70 Discussion List

The OCA Alliance Technical Committee operates a public discussion list for AES70. Anyone can post to the list - no membership is needed. Posts will be read and, if necessary, answered by members of the Technical Committee. Questions and comments are invited on all AES70-related topics. Newbies and experts are all welcome.

  • To post to the list, send email to aes70support@lists.ocalliance.com
